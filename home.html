DKF's Grasslands Survival Pack is an "all-in-one" pack for Minecraft which aims at creating a whole new experience, making the game more survival, roleplay, hard and beautiful at once.

Introducing GSP

  1. Hard and immersive survival
  2. Brand new gameplay/mechanics
  3. Restrictive roleplay rules
  4. Beauty of nature enhanced
  5. A lot of contents, all survival related,
    and without overloading

"I wanted to feel like surviving the wilderness, finding berries, building campfires to fight the cold, feel like the game is really hard and takes long to progress through. I wanted to find twigs among the leaves in the beginning, struggle for food, grow multiple crops, new minerals, new biomes, a more dangerous/beautiful nature and real hordes of zombies at night."

For those who want...

  1. ... a hard survival experience
  2. ... realistic mechanics
  3. ... to actually feel like being a survivor
  4. ... more exploration and RP
  5. ... to rediscover Minecraft

Every mod/plugin is configured, balanced, fixed and meant to be more than just a feature, but a real mechanic fitted to the gameplay. This is why i made a Survival Guide on this website, some of the mods are working really differently than they are expected to.

I was greatly inspired by Phedran and her modpack "Life in the woods", which i took from a base to create GSP. So, if you know this modpack you won't be too lost and if you do not, you should check it out !

Examples of features
  • Be careful your temperature, your thirst and your oxygen level.
  • You can't chop wood with your hands any more, so find twigs in the leaves and craft planks from it.
  • Finaly campfires ! One of them does not require coal which is useful during the first days.
  • You can climb up the walls, make charged jumps, crawl, slide but be REALLY careful with your exhaustion that makes your hunger drop faster.
  • Items have a certain weight and your inventory is limited to 7 slots (Make a backpack !).
  • The blocks are 10 times harder to break.
  • A lot of new biomes, minerals, meal recipes, fruits and crops to grow.
  • You begin with 6 hearts max and increase by getting levels. Health regeneration is really slower.
  • You have a skill tree and you can choose among 3 classes at level 8.
  • Real hordes of zombies at night. They are smarter too, they're attracted by the lights, smell your blood trails, climb the ladders...
  • Villagers have new skins, you can chat with them, make friends, hire guards, get married, have children and even a lot more.
  • You can only craft wooden tools, you need to use the new crafting tables to use stone. And you need a multi-block smeltery to craft metal tools.
  • You lose all your stuff when you die. Although, your backpack is dropped as usual with its content if you have one.
  • The environment is way more immersive, you can hear the wind blowing and see some leaves flying away, insects, quicksands, poisonous plants, wonderful biomes and a lot more...
  • Sleeping is disabled, you can only set a spawn point.
  • Days are 30% longer and nights are 10%
  • Darkness is darker (Gamma is decreased)
  • You are slowed down depeding on the type of terrain you're on.
  • New furnitures like lamps, tables, chairs...
  • Choose the size of the Biome o'plenty biomes easily.

Including a lot but so easy to install

GSP includes an auto-installer. You don't even need to open your .minecraft folder yourself. The installation is made as easy as possible, just follow the steps.

GSP also allows you to install Optifine, the Shader mod and some shaders, really easily even if they are not included.
Also, a sound pack is implemented to increase the immersion.

Play solo, but not single player !

GSP is a mod AND plugin pack for Minecraft. And as it uses plugins, it's meant to be played on server only. But you can play solo of course, and thanks to the manager, you can launch Minecraft and a server at the same time without any knowledge, in one click.

So do not launch a Singleplayer game. It won't crash, but as the plugins are not active the game will lack some of its main features. It is one of the cons, but a pro too, because it's ready to be played with friends without being worried about any complicated manipulation.
